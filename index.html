Welcome to Extravaganza 2.0, the second edition of the cultural fest organized by Government Engineering College Vaishali! Following the success of our first fest, we’re back with even more excitement and creativity. Extravaganza 2.0 offers a platform to showcase talent and foster connections across colleges. With a wide range of events in music, dance, drama, and art, we invite everyone to participate and celebrate together. Join us for a vibrant cultural experience filled with creativity, energy, and passion. Let’s make Extravaganza 2.0 a fest to remember!
